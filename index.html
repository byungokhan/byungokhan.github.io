About Me

I am a senior researcher at the Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI) and an associate professor at the University of Science and Technology (UST). I received my Bachelor's degree in computer science and engineering from Chung-Ang university in Seoul, South Korea. I obtained both my master's degree in robotics and ph.d. in computer science from the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) in Daejeon, South Korea. My research interests include computer vision, robot learning, and machine learning.

Our lab at UST is recruiting students for Master's, Ph.D., and integrated programs starting in the Spring semester of 2025! We welcome passionate and self-motivated students to apply. If you have any questions, please feel free to email me. [ byungok.han[at]etri.re.kr ] [ LVR Lab link ]
