About Me

I am ByungOk Han, a Senior Researcher at the Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI) and an Assistant Professor at the University of Science and Technology (UST). I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Chung-Ang University in Seoul, South Korea. I obtained both my Master's degree in Robotics and Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) in Daejeon, South Korea. My research interests include Computer Vision, Robotics, and Machine Learning.
